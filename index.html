Individual Location Summaries

🥇 Great Budworth (94/100) - Historic Village Perfection

The clear winner for character and exclusivity

✅ Strengths

  • Historic perfection with Grade I listed church
  • Conservation area protection
  • Properties with 1.2-1.5 acres currently available
  • Population of just 302 ensures exclusivity
  • Authentic estate village character

❌ Considerations

  • 40+ minutes to Manchester Airport
  • Limited daily amenities due to small size
  • Moderate transport links to London
  • May feel isolated for some

Best For: Those prioritizing heritage, character, and exclusivity above convenience

Available Properties: Large detached house in conservation area, 1.2+ acres, nearly 3000 sq ft; "The Farthings" with electric gates and 1.5 acres

Excellent availability with historic market town character

✅ Strengths

  • Excellent current availability (£1.075M example)
  • Historic market town with thriving high street
  • Expansion potential (additional 2 acres available)
  • Award-winning villages in area
  • Good amenities and services

❌ Considerations

  • Further from airports than Golden Triangle
  • London travel requires connections
  • Not as exclusive as Great Budworth
  • Some tourism impact

Best For: Families wanting village life with good services and expansion opportunities

Available Properties: Eaton village property with 1+ acre, 3000+ sq ft, additional 2 acres and workshop available
